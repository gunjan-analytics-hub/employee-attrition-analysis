Analyzing employee attrition to identify trends and improve retention strategies using data

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze patterns and trends in employee attrition
- Identify key factors influencing employee turnover
- Build interactive dashboards for better visualization
- Provide data-driven insights for HR decision-making

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
- **SQL** – Data extraction and querying  
- **Power BI** – Data visualization and dashboard creation  
- **Excel** – Data cleaning and preprocessing
- **Pandas** - Data Mainpulation and Analysis
- **Seaborn** - Data Visualization
- **Matplotlib** - Data Visualization

---

## 📂 Dataset
- The dataset contains employee-related information such as:
  - Age
  - Department
  - Job Role
  - Gender
  - Salary
  - Job Satisfaction
  - Overtime
  - Work Life Balance
  - Years At Company
  - Performance Rating
  - Attrition (Yes/No)

---

## 🔍 Key Analysis Performed
- Attrition rate calculation  
- Department-wise attrition analysis  
- Salary and attrition relationship  
- Job satisfaction vs attrition  
- Overtime impact on attrition  

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- Employees with **low job satisfaction** show higher attrition  
- **Overtime** is strongly associated with increased attrition  
- Certain departments have significantly higher turnover rates  
- Lower salary ranges tend to have higher attrition
